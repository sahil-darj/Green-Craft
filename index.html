About Us

Building India's Future with Quality Stainless Steel

25+ years of excellence in stainless steel manufacturing

Excellence in Stainless Steel Manufacturing Since 1998

Green Craft has been at the forefront of India's stainless steel industry for over 25 years. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver stainless steel products that meet the highest international standards.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are equipped with the latest technology. We produce a comprehensive range of stainless steel products including plates, bhojanthals, compartment plates, and decorative dining products that enhance dining experiences across India.

With a wide product range serving households, hotels, and restaurants across 50+ cities, we maintain our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our ISO certification and multiple industry awards stand testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

Charting our growth from a local manufacturer to a national stainless steel leader

1998

Foundation & Vision

Green Craft was established with a vision to revolutionize India's stainless steel dining products industry. Started with a small facility producing premium plates and thalis.

2005

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO certification, marking our commitment to international quality standards. Expanded product range to include decorative and compartment plates.

2010

Technology Upgrade

Invested in advanced technology for mirror polish finish and precision manufacturing. Introduced automated quality control systems and expanded product range.

2015

National Expansion

Expanded distribution to 30+ cities across India. Launched specialized product lines for hotels and restaurants, establishing national presence.

2020

Sustainable Manufacturing

Implemented green manufacturing practices, reducing environmental impact. Achieved wider product range while maintaining environmental responsibility.

2024

Industry Leadership

Recognized as one of India's leading stainless steel plate manufacturers. Serving 50+ cities with cutting-edge stainless steel solutions for dining and hospitality.
